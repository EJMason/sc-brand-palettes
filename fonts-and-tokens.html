Corrected recommendation (per the research): don't pair Figtree with Atkinson — two workhorse sans-serifs read as ambiguous ("which one is the brand voice?"). The right rule is one workhorse + one display, or a single font. Two clean paths — (1) Figtree (UI/body) + a display like Fraunces or Bricolage Grotesque for headings + a mono for numbers (the friendly route, best fit for SalesCaddie); or (2) Atkinson Hyperlegible Next (body) + a serif display if "the readable product" is the brand story.
