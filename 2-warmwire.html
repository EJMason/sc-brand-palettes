2 · Warmwire — friendly & rounded

header: Fraunces (serif) · body: Figtree

Says: Human and warm.

Warmwire is the most human of the six – and the closest to what SalesCaddie actually does. Large 18px corners, pill buttons, generous spacing, and a Fraunces serif headline do real work here: they create warmth you can feel rather than just describe. Magenta leads with confidence, yellow adds optimism, teal keeps the whole thing from overheating. The result reads like a person made it: approachable, generous, a little joyful. That's exactly the feeling a handwritten note and a gift are supposed to create.
